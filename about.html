The Person Behind the Passport

Hi, I'm Suzy

I'm a travel blogger based in Lisbon, though I'm rarely there long enough to water the plants. What started as a one-way ticket and a half-finished spreadsheet of "someday" destinations turned into a life spent chasing street food stalls, hidden coastlines, and the kind of conversations you only have with strangers on night trains.

These days I split my time between writing from tiny cafés in whatever city I've landed in and packing a bag that's somehow always slightly too full. This blog is where I keep the stories that don't fit in a caption — the wrong turns that became the best part of the trip, the meals worth the layover, and the places that quietly rearranged how I see the world.
